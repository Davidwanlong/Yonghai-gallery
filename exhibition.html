Exhibitions

Hebei Oil Painter Gallery was established in the beginning of 2000 at 116 West Street, Shijiazhuang City, the capital of Hebei Province, with a business area of more than 60 square meters. The painting gallery hired Fei Zheng, director of the Chinese Oil Painting Society and a senior artist, as the consultant, the director of the Chinese Oil Painting Society and the president of Hebei Oil Painting Society, the young and middle-aged artist Sun Gang as the artistic director, and the middle-aged oil painter Wang Yonghai as the artistic host. It brings together influential domestic artists such as Fei Zheng, Sun Gang, Xu Fuhou, Spiegel, Jia Xiangguo, Zhao Wenhua, Wang Tao, Niu Yungong, Nanfang, etc. Agent works are fine masterpieces of artists and national authoritative exhibitions selected, award-winning works, with high collection value. The gallery adheres to the principle of both academic and artistic importance, and provides collectors and society with high-grade and high-quality original oil painting works. Standardized operation and honest service are the gallery's consistent code of conduct, and our goal is to strive for a first-class gallery in China.

In September 2001, at the invitation of Liu Gang, general manager of "Shanghai Painter's Village", Hebei Oil Painters Gallery moved to Shanghai and settled in the painter's Village Room 2003. The works of Hebei oil Painters Gallery are displayed in the entrance room of the painter's Village exhibition hall, which is particularly eye-catching. The works of the mirror are asked by Liu Gang, and they were soon purchased by friends in Singapore. In November of the same year, he participated in the "Shanghai Art Fair" and "Shanghai Art Fair Gallery Invitation Exhibition" for three consecutive years. Many of his works are collected at home and abroad.
